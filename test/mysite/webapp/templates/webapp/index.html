WHAT WILL IT TAKES TO SAVE WHAT REMAINS?

RISE

"Save what remains". SHD Agents are activated as part of Order Directive 51 to counter-act the growing violence and rebuild what was left in the quarantined Manhattan.

RAVAGE

Manhattan and other parts of New York are now under Martial Law. Increasing number of hostiles pose a threat to the safety and reclamation of the City. Those who resists or fight back will be shot.

CLEANSE

New York Central Park is now converted into massive burial ground. Most bodies that are contaminated are being flamed to avoid any further leak of infection. "Precaution is advised".

BETRAY

Enter the Darkzone, the most dangerous part of Manhattan swarming with different Hostiles, and fight together with your teammates or disavow the Division by being rogue for the personal cause.
